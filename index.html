HOW TO PLAY

Controls

  • ↑↓← → - Steer your car
  • A / Z - Accelerate
  • S / X - Brake
  • G - Toggle Gear (LOW/HI)

Gears

  • LOW: Low speed, fast acceleration
  • HI: High speed, slower acceleration

Objective

  • Complete each track within the time limit
  • Finish in top 3 to advance to next track
  • 3 difficulty levels with 5 tracks each
  • Avoid crashes - they cost precious seconds

ABOUT F-1 RACE

Original Game: 1984 - HAL Laboratory & Nintendo

Platform: Family Computer (Famicom)

Web Version: 2024

Features

  • Top-down racing action
  • Two-speed manual transmission
  • 3 difficulty levels
  • 5 unique tracks per difficulty
  • AI opponents
  • Collision physics

Game Description

F-1 Race is one of the first attempts to bring Formula 1 excitement to home consoles. Race on tracks inspired by world capitals and compete against AI drivers.
